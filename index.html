The Democratic Republic of India

The Democratic republic of India is a beautiful country situated in South Asia,It's capital is NEW DELHI, and it's largest city is BOMBAY

NEW DELHI

New Delhi is the second larget city of India by population and have been capital of India under variour rulers, it dates back to Samrat Bharat,when Indraprasth(DELHI) was capital of India. Indraprastha or Delhi had played a crucial role in history of India.

BOMBAY/MUMBAI

Mumbai formerly known as Bombay is financial capital of India, it is the most populated city of India. It is situated and capital of Maharastra.
